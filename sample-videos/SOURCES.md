## Generated sample videos

`classpulse-sample-classroom-A-video0109.mp4` (24s) and `classpulse-sample-classroom-B-video0107.mp4` (18s)
were generated from frames in the Roboflow dataset
[renu-vpzia/student-behavior-xeopo](https://universe.roboflow.com/renu-vpzia/student-behavior-xeopo) (CC BY 4.0):
sequences video0109/cam01 and video0107/cam01, frames sorted by number, un-stretched to 1280x720, 5 fps.
Source frames were sampled from the original lesson recording (~1 frame per ~0.7s), so motion is time-lapse-like.

Other useful CC-BY sources found:
- **EngageNY** YouTube channel — dozens of short (~2-3 min) real US classroom clips, all CC-BY
  (e.g. https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1WQCWF7ENfI)
- "Watching Teachers Work" (New America, 1h46m) — https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=yKFwkvsaM8Y
